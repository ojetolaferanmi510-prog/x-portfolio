About

Engineer. Builder. Problem-solver.

I turn fuzzy product ideas into reliable software — with a bias for clean APIs, sharp UI, and systems that hold up under real users.

I’m a full-stack developer specializing in Web3, fintech, and e-commerce. My work sits at the intersection of product thinking and solid engineering: clear data models, secure auth, thoughtful UX, and deployable backends.

Whether it’s a DeFi dashboard, a lending flow, or a utility SaaS, I care about the details that make software feel intentional — loading states, empty states, error paths, and the boring reliability work that keeps things online.

I studied Computer Science at Precious Cornerstone University, and I keep shipping side projects and client work that stretch the stack: Node, MongoDB, modern frontends, and on-chain integrations when the product needs them.
